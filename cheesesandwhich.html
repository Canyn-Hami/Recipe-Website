Grilled Cheese Sandwich

The ultimate comfort food! Easy to cook but tasty.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread

  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese (or ½ cup shredded)

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise (optional)

  • 2 slices of tomato

  • A few thin onion rings

  • A pinch of ground black pepper

    •

Procedure:

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.

  2. Spread mayo on the other side (optional, for crispier crust).

  3. Layer cheese, tomato, onion, and a dash of pepper between bread (butter sides facing out).

  4. Grill in a skillet on medium heat until golden brown and cheese melts (about 2–3 min per side).

  5. Slice into triangle shape.

  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Note: Grilled Cheese Sandwich is a crispy, buttery toast filled with melted cheese—simple, warm, and comforting.
