Chicken Pea Salad

Try this spaghetti aglio e olio recipe once, and you'll make it over and over. It uses simple ingredients, it cooks in 20 minutes, and it's totally delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded or diced

  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed

  • ½ cup cucumber, diced

  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

    •

Procedure:

  1. Shred or dice the cooked chicken.

  2. Drain and rinse the canned chickpeas.

  3. Dice the cucumber and halve the cherry tomatoes.

  4. In a bowl, combine chicken, chickpeas, cucumber, and tomatoes.

  5. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the mixture.

  6. Toss gently and season with salt and pepper. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Note: Chicken & Chickpea Salad is a quick, healthy mix of chicken, chickpeas, fresh veggies, and a simple lemon-olive oil dressing—perfect for a light meal!
