Quesadilllas

Simple tortilla filled with cheese and veggies, then grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 flour tortillas

  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)

  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, sliced

  • 1/4 cup onions, sliced

  • 1 tsp oil or butter

    •

Procedure:

  1. Heat a pan and add a little oil or butter.

  2. Place one tortilla in the pan and add cheese and veggies on top.

  3. Cover with the second tortilla and cook until golden on both sides.

  4. Cut into wedges and serve warm with salsa or sour cream.

Note: The tortilla is typically warmed, filled, folded in half, and then cooked until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is slightly crispy.
