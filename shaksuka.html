Shaksuka

A delicious and easy dish with poached eggs in a spicy tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 bell pepper, chopped

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (or 1 can diced tomatoes)

  • 4 eggs

  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste

    •

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion, garlic, and bell pepper until soft.

  2. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, and paprika. Simmer until thickened.

  3. Make small wells in the sauce and crack in the eggs.

  4. Cover and cook until eggs are just set (about 5–7 minutes).

  5. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

  6. Serve warm with crusty bread.

Note: Shakshuka is a flavorful dish of poached eggs in a spiced tomato and pepper sauce—perfect for breakfast or any meal.
