Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Try this spaghetti aglio e olio recipe once, and you'll make it over and over. It uses simple ingredients, it cooks in 20 minutes, and it's totally delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti

  • 4 Cloves Garlic, thinly sliced

  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil

  • 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes (adjust to taste)

  • Salt to taste

  • Fresh Parsley, Chopped (optional)

  • Grated Parmesan Cheese (optional)

    •

Procedure:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions (8-10 minutes). Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain.

  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and saut√© until golden brown, being careful not to burn it.

  3. Add the red pepper flakes and cook for another 30 seconds.

  4. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the garlic and oil mixture. Toss well to coat, adding reserved pasta water as needed.

  5. Season with salt, and garnish with fresh parsley and Parmesan cheese (optional).

  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Note: This dish is perfect for a quick dinner or lunch, and can easily be adjusted with your preferred level of spice!
