Quesadillas

Simple tortilla filled with cheese and veggies, then grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 flour tortillas

  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or mix)

  • ½ cup chopped bell peppers

  • ½ cup chopped onions

  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Salsa or sour cream (for serving)

    •

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.

  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet.

  3. Sprinkle cheese, bell peppers, onions, salt, and pepper evenly.

  4. Top with the second tortilla.

  5. Cook until the bottom is golden, then flip and cook the other side.

  6. Slice into wedges and serve with salsa or sour cream.

Note: Quesadillas are quick, cheesy tortillas filled with veggies or meat, grilled until crispy—perfect for a fast and tasty meal.
