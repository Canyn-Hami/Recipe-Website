Welcome to Our Recipe Website

Welcome to the delightful culinary journey brought to you by BSIT-1B! This website is more than just a collection of recipes; it’s a showcase of our creativity, teamwork, and passion for technology and good food. Here, you’ll find a variety of delicious recipes, from classic comfort food to exciting new flavors, all carefully curated to make your cooking experience both easy and enjoyable.

As part of our BSIT-1B course project, this website demonstrates our skills in web development and design, serving as a testament to what we’ve learned and created together. We invite you to explore, cook, and share the joy of food with us!

Our Project History "Gourmet Guide"

Phase 1: Recipe Selection & Testing

The team chose ten recipes, considering ease of preparation, affordability, and broad appeal. Recipes were tested and refined to ensure accuracy and feasibility for home cooks.

Phase 2: Content Creation

Recipes were written out with clear instructions and ingredient lists. The website's text content was created during this phase.

Phase 3: Website Development

The team built a basic website using HTML to showcase the recipes. The website's design was simple and focused on clear presentation of recipe information. The URL suggests the use of a hosting service (GitHub Pages).
